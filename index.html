Apply for a Permit

Follow these simple steps to submit your permit application in New Rochelle

1

Go to CitySquared Permit System

Access the online permit portal and create your account.

üí° Pro Tip

If you had an account with the old system, use the same email address to preserve your historical applications.

  • Click "Login" in top right corner
  • Register if you don't have an account
  • Use same email as old system to preserve historical applications
Go to CitySquared Portal
2

Search for New Rochelle

Locate the City of New Rochelle in the municipality search.

  • Search for "City of New Rochelle" in the Municipality field
  • Select the correct municipality from search results
3

Apply for Permit

Start your permit application process.

  • Click "Apply for" > "Permit"
  • Choose "Buildings" department
  • Select your specific permit type
‚ö†Ô∏è Important

Make sure to select the correct permit type as this affects the required documentation and review process.

4

Property Search

Find and add your property to the application.

üí° Search Tip

Less is more! When searching, only enter the property number and first few letters of the street name for better results.

  • Enter property number and street name
  • Use minimal information for better search results
  • Add the property to your application
5

Add Contacts

Include all relevant parties for your project.

  • Add property owner information
  • Add applicant details
  • Include contractors and professionals
  • Add architect or engineer if applicable
6

Complete Building Details

Provide comprehensive information about your project.

  • Enter project scope and description
  • Specify construction type and materials
  • Include square footage and dimensions
  • Add any special requirements or notes
7

Add Insurance Information

Upload required insurance certificates and documentation.

  • Add insurance information for all parties
  • Upload insurance certificates (PDF format)
  • Ensure certificates are current and valid
  • Include liability and workers' compensation as required
8

Upload Plans & Documents

Submit all required drawings, plans, and supporting materials.

  • Upload architectural plans (PDF format)
  • Include structural drawings if required
  • Add engineering reports or specifications
  • Submit any additional supporting documents
9

Review Application

Double-check all sections before final submission.

‚ö†Ô∏è Before You Submit

Review all sections carefully. Once submitted, some changes may require amendments or additional fees.

  • Check all sections for completeness
  • Verify contact information is accurate
  • Confirm all required documents are uploaded
  • Review building details and project scope
  • Edit any sections if needed
10

Submit Application

Finalize and submit your permit application.

  • Click "Submit" to finalize your application
  • View application summary after submission
  • Save your confirmation number for tracking
  • Note: You can still add contacts/documents after submission
11

Track & Manage

Monitor your application and handle post-submission tasks.

  • Access your dashboard to track application progress
  • Respond promptly to any city requests or comments
  • Apply for amendments if project changes occur
  • Request Certificate of Occupancy when construction is complete
  • Pay any required fees through the system
